Angel Program

Do you frequently fly with Halo Airlines? Join our Angel Program for frequent flyers!

Angel points are received for every flight you purchase. These points can be accumulated to be used on another flight. When the points are used, they translate to money which is removed from the final cost of the flight. Once points are used, they are removed from your angel point count.


The amount of points received for every flight will vary.


On the anniversary of you joining the Angel Program, you will be gifted 50 angel points. Every year that you remain an Angel flyer, the gifted points will increase.


As an Angel flyer, you also will receive notifications of the cheapest flights that are currently available. You can also be the first to board all flights with Halo Airlines!


You will receive a physical card to keep track of points. The number of the card will also be used for online transactions.


Contact us at angelprogramhalo@email.com with any questions or concerns about the Angel Program
